About

Alumni Connect bridges the gap between past and present, fostering meaningful relationships, collaboration, and lifelong engagement within our alumni community.

  • September 2024

    Conceptualization and Early Engagement

    The journey of the Alumni Connect website began in September 2024, driven by a dedicated team consisting of Divyadarshini R, Dhiksha Shrivruthi G, and Abeak J. The initial focus was on connecting with alumni, understanding their needs, and gathering valuable insights to shape the direction of the platform. This phase laid the foundation for the platform’s purpose—creating a space for ongoing engagement and collaboration among alumni.

  • October 2024

    Design Thinking and Prototyping

    In October 2024, the team applied design thinking principles to enhance the development process. By focusing on empathy, ideation, and iteration, the team was able to create a more refined and user-centered approach to the website. Prototypes were developed and tested, incorporating feedback from initial user interactions, ensuring that the platform would meet the diverse needs of the alumni community.

  • November 2024

    Launch of Minimum Viable Website

    With key functionalities in place, the team launched the minimum viable website (MVW) in November 2024. This version featured essential tools for alumni to connect, share information, and access important resources. Although the website was still in its early stages, it provided a solid foundation for further development and served as a testing ground for continuous improvement based on user feedback.

  • January 2025

    Full Website Launch and Expansion

    The final phase culminated in January 2025, with the launch of a fully-fledged and feature-rich website. After months of development, testing, and refinement, the Alumni Connect platform was ready to provide a comprehensive, seamless experience for alumni. The new site included advanced features, tools for deeper networking, and opportunities for alumni to engage in meaningful, long-term connections with the community.
